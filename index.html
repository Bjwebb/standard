360 Giving Data Standard

The 360 Giving project has set out a five-year ambition for open philanthropy in the UK: to see trusts and foundations reporting on the grants they give - supporting more informed, effective and transparent grant making across the country.

To help join up grant information from different trusts and foundations, we've been developing a prototype data standard.

These pages document that development work. We've now moved on and have put together a final version of the standard. You can find that documented at http://docs.threesixtygiving.org
